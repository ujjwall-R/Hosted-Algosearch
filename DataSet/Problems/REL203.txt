

A couple is on Honeymoon and they want to visit every place in the city instead of staying back at hotel. And they want to travel in such a way so that they spend least time in travelling. They starts travelling from hotel and you are supposed to find out minimum time such that they visit every place. The places in a city are connected by bidirectional road and there is unique path between any pair of place. They can travel through road any number of time such that they must have to visit all place in minimum possible time.
You will be given N places with the road and travelling time among places. The couple start from hotel which is situated at place 1.

Input

First line contains T, the number of test cases.
Each test case consists of one integer N, number of places in city.
Each next N-1 lines consist of three integers U,V and W denotes there is road between U and V. W is the time to travel from U to V or vice-versa. 

Output

For each test case print the minimum time require to visit all the places in city.

Constraints

1 <= T <= 10
1 <= N <= 20000
1 <= U,V <= N
1 <= W <= 10^9

Example
Input:
1
6
1 2 1
1 3 4
2 5 2
2 6 50
1 4 5

Output:
73

Explanation

Following is the travelling path of couple:
1->4->1->3->1->2->5->2->6
