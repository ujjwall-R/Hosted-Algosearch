
Tony is interested in Wayne tech stocks. Before buying the shares he gathered Wayne share price for past NN days and was analyzing when he got Steve call for emergency Avengers meeting. Before leaving he asked JARVIS to tell him the maximum profit that could have been attained from single share in past NN days. 
Can you tell the answer given by JARVIS ?
Input:

First line will contain NN, number of days. 
Second line contains NN integers where ith integer represent share price on that day i.e. price[i]

Output:
Ouput single integer i.e. maximum profit.
Constraints

1≤N≤106
1≤price[i]≤109

Subtasks
Subtask #1(100 points): Original Constraints
Sample Input:
5
100 300 200 400 500

Sample Output:
500

EXPLANATION:
Day 1: Buy
Day 2: Sell, Net profit = 200
Day 3: Buy
Day 4: Sell, Net profit = 500
