
Prajit was trying to solve a tricky problem .As we all know he is a noob he failed to solve that and he gave up for the day.He left the problem and went to sleep.Can you solve it?
There is a number NN.We need to find total unset bits of that number.
Input:
First line contains TT,Test cases.
Second line contains a single integer NN .
Output:
Print a single integer.
Constraints:

1≤T≤1000
1≤N≤1018

Example Input:
2
10
1
Example Output:
2
0
