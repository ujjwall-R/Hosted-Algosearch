
Teacher brought a fruit basket for three students. The basket contains only Apples, Mangoes and Oranges. Student A knows a value aa, the total number of Apples and Mangoes in the Basket, B knows a value bb, the total number of Mangoes and Oranges in the basket and Student C knows a value cc, the total number of Oranges and Apples in the Basket. Since the teacher brought it he knows a value d , the total number of fruits in the basket. You have to determine the exact number of Apples , Mangoes and Oranges in the basket separately.
Input:

First line will contain T, number of testcases. Then the testcases follow. 
Each testcase contains of a single line of input, four integers a,b,c,d. 

Output:
For each testcase, output in a single line the number of Apples , Mangoes and Oranges in this order.
Constraints

1≤T≤100
0≤a≤1000
0≤b≤1000
0≤c≤1000
0≤d≤1000 

-The Solution always exisits
Sample Input:
2

7 9 8 12
3 8 7 9
Sample Output:
3 4 5

1 2 6
