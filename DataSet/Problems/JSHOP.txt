
Jon opened his new shop and he sells N number of items.
Price of each item is represented using an array P where Pi represents the price of item i.
A customer always buys 3 items of the lowest price from his shop.
Find out the profit Jon can make from that customer. It is guaranteed that his shop has at least 3 items.
Input Format

First line will contain T, number of testcases. Each testcase contains of two lines of input.
First line contains N which represents the number of items in Jon's shop.
Second line contains N space separated integers P1,P2,P3,....,PN which represents the price of each item.

Output Format
For each testcase, output in a single line answer the profit Jon makes from those 3 items.
Constraints

1≤T≤1000
3≤N≤105
1≤Ai≤103


                        Sample Input 1
                        





2
4
9 4 10 5 
6
6 2 6 4 2 10 



                        Sample Output 1
                        





18
8


Explanation
First Test Case
3 items the customer chose = 9 + 4 + 5 = 18
Second Test Case
3 items the customer chose = 2 + 4 + 2 = 8
