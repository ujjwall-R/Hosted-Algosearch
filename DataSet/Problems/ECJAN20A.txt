
Chefina is Chef's 8-year-old daughter. She studies in the 3rd standard. Recently her class teacher introduced her to a new shape
i.e. a Triangle. Chefina being a curious student wants to know whether the angles she says can form a triangle or not. Since today is Republic day the school declared a holiday, so it is up to Chef to feed Chefina's curious brain. But Chef is busy in
the preparation for the Republic Day Cake, hence he wants you to help Chefina with her curiosity.
Input:

First-line will contain TT, the number of test cases. Then the test cases follow. 
Each test case contains three inputs, integer A,B,CA,B,C 

Output:
Print "YES" or "NO" without inverted commas. The answer to each test case should be in a new line. Print the answer in CAPITALCAPITAL only else solution will not be accepted.
Constraints

1≤T≤1051≤T≤105
1≤A,B,C<180

Sample Input:
3
60 60 60
90 90 90
45 45 90

Sample Output:
YES
NO
YES

Explaination
In the first and third cases, a triangle is possible but in the second case, there cannot be 3 right angles.
