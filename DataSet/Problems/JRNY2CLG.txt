
Mayur is hell excited as his college is re-opening physically on 21st21st March. As he lives a bit far away from the college, he needs some time for travelling.
Given the starting date & the duration of his journey, determine whether he will be able to reach the college before 21st21st March or not.
Input Format

There is a single line containing two integers S and D.
S denotes the starting date. It is guaranteed that the given date is from the month of March of the same year.
D denotes the duration (i.e., the number of days) of the whole journey.

Output Format
Print YES if he will reach the college before 21st March, else print NO.
You may print every letter in any case you want (so, for example, the strings yEs, yes, Yes and YES are all recognized as a positive answer).
Constraints

1≤S≤31
0≤D≤20


                        Sample Input 1
                        





15 3



                        Sample Output 1
                        





YES


Explanation
He will travel from 15th to 17th March, & reach the college on 18th March.

                        Sample Input 2
                        





10 11



                        Sample Output 2
                        





NO


Explanation
He will travel from 10th to 20th March, & reach the college on 21st March. But it was instructed to reach before 21st March.
