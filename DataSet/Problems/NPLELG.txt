

Aniket has just won a contest and will recieve a prize worth  w  rupees. He wishes to buy three items he loves having cost  x , y and z . If he can buy the three items he becomes Happy else he is Sad. Given w, x, y and z. Can you tell if he is Happy or Sad. Okay.


Input
First line contains 4 integers denoting w,x,y and z respectively.

Output

Print "Happy" if Aniket can buy the three items else print "Sad".

Constraints

0 ≤ w,x,y,z ≤ 108

Example
Input 1:
10 2 3 4

Output 1:
Happy


Input 2:
10 3 4 5

Output 2:
Sad
