
Chef asks Chefina for a date . But Chef is a hardworking guy and has a value for money so he already pre-planned about his date and fixed a budget to spend from his savings . 
Given a fixed budget BB and an array AA[ ] of size N for the amount of N expenses .
You have to calculate the total amount and check whether the date costs him beyond his fixed budget . If the total amount goes beyond budget then print “YES” otherwise “NO” .
Input:

First line will contain T, number of testcases. Then the testcases follow. 
First line of each testcase contains of a single line of input, two integers N,B. 
Second line of each testcase contains N integers A[0],A[1],...,A[N−1] separated by a single space.

Output:
For each testcase, output in a single line answer YES or NO .
Constraints

1≤T≤100
1≤N≤104
1≤A[i]≤104

Sample Input:
2
2 5
4 2
4 15
4 3 5 2 

Sample Output:
YES

NO
