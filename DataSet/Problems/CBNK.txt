
Chef’s school has a hectic timetable. There are NN periods in a day. In the ithith period, Chef is supposed to study subject ai. Since all the subjects are not related to cooking, Chef hates some subjects. Formally, given two integers x and y, Chef hates a subject if for that subject  ai, x ≤ ai ≤ y.
Find the number of periods Chef has to attend, if he bunks all the periods of subjects he hates.
Input:

First line will contain T, number of testcases. Then the testcases follow. 
Each testcase contains of a two lines of input, first line contains three integers N,x,y. Second line contains N integers ai. 

Output:
For each testcase, output the answer for that testcase in a single line.
Constraints

1≤T≤100
1≤N≤2∗105
1≤x≤y≤109
1≤ai≤109

Sample Input:
1
5 5 9
4 3 6 10 1

Sample Output:
4

EXPLANATION:
Chef has to attend periods 1st,2nd,4th and 5th.
