
Chef and his friend got bored with their daily routine. So, they decided to play a game.
Chef’s friend gives Chef sum SS of first nn natural numbers.
S=1+2+...nS=1+2+...n
He told Chef to find a number P such that, 
P=13+23+….n3
Can you help Chef find P?
Input:

The first line of the input contains T denoting the number of test cases. The description of T test cases follows.
The first line of each test case contains an integer S.

Output:
For each test case, output a single integer on a new line the value of P.
Constraints

1≤T≤10
2≤S≤104

Sample Input:
5
10
3
6
15
1

Sample Output:
100
9
36
225
1  

EXPLANATION:
For first test case n=4 ,
S=1+2+3+4=10
P=13+23+33+43=100
