
You have given a sentence as a String SS. Your task is to print the Sorted order of lengths of all the words present in the given sentence. A sentence is a string comprised of multiple words and words are separated with spaces.
Input:
The single line contains a sentence PP.
Output:
Print the lengths of the words in the Sorted order.
Constraints

1 <= Length of S <= 107107
1 <= Number of words present in a Sentence <= 105

Sample Input:
Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix

Sample Output:
2 3 3 3 5 5 6 7

EXPLANATION:
In the given Sentence there are 8 Words of Lengths 5, 6, 3, 3, 5, 2, 3 , 7. Sorted order is [2,3,3,3,5,5,6,7].
