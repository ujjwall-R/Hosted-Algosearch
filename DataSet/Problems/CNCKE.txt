
Chef has to prepare cakes for his bakery. Each cake requires aa number of eggs, bb gms of flour and c ml of milk. The bakery has a stock of x number of eggs, y kgs of flour and z litres of milk. Calculate the total number of cakes that can be prepared. 
Input:

First line of input consists of number of test cases T
Then testcases follow, each line of testcase inputs 6 variables a,b,c,x,y and z.

Output:
Print the number of cakes which can be formed for each testcase. 
Constraints

1≤T≤1000
1≤a,b,c,x,y,z≤106

Sample Input:
3
1 200 200 5 1 1 
2 100 100 4 200 2
3 3000 300 10 2 3

Sample Output:
5
2
0
