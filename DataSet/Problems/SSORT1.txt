
Chef has NN new recruits lined up. HiHi denotes height of ith recruit. Chef tells a positive integer K to you and asks you to arrange the recruits such that first K recruits are in increasing order of height, next K are in decreasing order of height and so on. Tell the new standing order of recruits.
Note: The last portion of recruits will be less than or equal to K.
Input:

First line will contain N, number of recruits.
Second line contains height of N recruits in inital standing order. 
Third line contains integer K.

Output:
For each testcase, output in a new order of recruits,
Constraints

1≤N≤106
1≤Hi≤109 
1≤K≤N

Subtasks

Subtask #1(100 points): Original Constraints

Sample Input:
5
1 2 3 7 5
2

Sample Output:
1 2 7 3 5
