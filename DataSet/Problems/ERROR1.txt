
Aman a college student is on his vacation and want to visit a place DD but before visiting DD he must have to visit BB and then C and only after visiting C he can visit D.
There are x routes for visiting B , y route for visiting C from B and z route for visiting D from C.
so you have to find the maximum possible route for visiting place D from the initial position.
Input:

First line will contain T, number of testcases. Then the testcases follow. 
Each testcase contains of a single line of input, three integers x,y,z. 

Output:
For each testcase, output in a single line the maximum possible routes for visiting D from initial position.
Constraints

1≤T≤100
1≤x,y,z≤20

Sample Input:
1
2 3 4

Sample Output:
24
