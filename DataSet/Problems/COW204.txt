

Chefu is studying English language and is obsessed with palindrome strings. Chefu also a good student and wants to earn the Prefecto, a badge of honer given to the most brillant mind of the class.


Since every one in the class want to earn the badge, teacher devices a task. She would give a number of strings(may or may not be palindrome) and ask the student to find the longest palindrome in that string from the begining.Help Chefu to earn the PrefctoPrefcto badge.

Input:

First-line will contain TT, the number of test cases. Then the test case follows. 
 Each test case contains a single input SS (Given String)

Output:
For each test case, print the length of the longest palindrome from the beginning 
Constraints

1≤T≤1000
1≤|S|≤103

Sample Input:
4
abbaxty
abcd
aaaaa
abxbatvaaaaaaaa

Sample Output:
4
1
5
5

Explanation
In case 1:Longest palindrome  from beginning string=abba, length=4

In case 2:Longest palindrome  from beginning string=a, length=1
