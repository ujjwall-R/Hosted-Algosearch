
Chef is checking his sons arithmetical skills by asking a simple question provided below.
Given two numbers AA and BB, if AA is greater then B , then output the difference of the two numbers and if B is greater then A output  product of the two numbers,
else if A and B both are equal output sum of the two numbers. 
chef's son is busy doing sometihing else so he asks you for help.
Input:

First line will contain T, number of testcases. Then the testcases follow. 
Each testcase contains of a single line of input, two integers A,B. 

Output:
For each testcase, output the required answer in a single line.
Constraints

1≤A≤1000
2≤B≤109

Sample Input:
2
2 2
2 3

Sample Output:
4

6
