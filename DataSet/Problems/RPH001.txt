
Ganesh Gaitonde, the don of Gopalmath, wants everything the way he likes. His true love Jojo  is however upset with his rude behavior.
Ganesh wants to impress Jojo with his skills, but Jojo is a maths freak.  She challenged Gaitonde to solve a mathematical problem. So he catches you, the best mathematician of gopalmath to solve the problem and you won`t be released until you solve it. 
Jojo asks Ganesh to reverse and add two strings.  Now solve the problem if you wanna be freed.
Input:
first line is test cases
followed by testcases, each line containg two numbers
Output:
For each testcase, output in a single line Integer value.
Constraints

1≤t≤100
1≤n≤ 2200 

Sample Input:
   1
   21 1

Sample Output:
   31

EXPLANATION:
   This above expression can be solved as below:
   12+1=13 reverse of 13 is 31
