
Rahul is new to mathematics and doesn't know how to add two integers. Will you help him?
Given two positivepositive integers, aa and bb, write a program to find the addition of a and b.   
Input:
2 positive integers, a and b, are given separated by space.
Output:
Output in a single line, the sum of a and b.
Constraints

1≤a,b≤104

Sample Input:
7 8
Sample Output:
15
EXPLANATION:
Given, 7 and 8, their sum is 15.
